Our story began one summer: when our founder, a junior high-school student, was searching for internships but seemed to find the process to be confusing and unclear. Many platforms lacked reliable information, and for students new to this space, finding opportunities felt overwhelming.

That moment led to a bigger realization: young people aren’t lost because they lack potential, but because they lack guidance, real experiences, and a safe space to explore. The problem was never ability, it was guidance

So, Bidaya was created to bridge that gap by connecting personal growth with real-world opportunities in one simple platform. Today, we help students turn small steps into a clear path toward purpose, education, and career.

What is
?

Bidaya is an all-in-one, globally accessible career guidance platform that empowers students to explore their path and take control of their future—guiding them from early exploration to final application through personalized support, integrated tools, and a seamless, interactive experience.

Our Mission

To transform uncertainty into clarity by guiding the youth to discover their purpose in the real world and equip them with the confidence, knowledge, and direction to shape their own path every step along the way.

Our Vision

To be able to reach young people all over the world and to become the guiding compass that leads them through a journey of self and career discovery. We strive to make every step they take a stride towards their future, helping them take every opportunity and every chance to find their purpose, build their career, and leave their mark.

Our Values

Who do we serve?

Our app aims to serve young individuals aged 15-25 who are searching for a purpose and want to match their career aspirations with their passions. By offering structured advice and sensory-friendly routines, we specifically serve neurodivergent people, such as those with ADHD, autism, or dyslexia.

WHY BIDAYA?

✦ For Individuals

Bidaya is a platform designed to be your own personalized compass, guiding you towards a purposeful future while making the process of figuring out where you’re going less stressful. Built with your convenience in mind, every feature aims to make your journey easier.

Not only does Bidaya connect you to every opportunity of interest, whether it’s an internship, course, or event, but it also simplifies the process of identifying the right opportunity for you. By studying your interests, habits, and hobbies, from AI chatbots to bucket list timelines, every input you log into the platform helps build your personalized profile, one that will make your journey clearer and gradually help you build your CV, making you stand out in the job market later on.

Bidaya also connects you to a community of like-minded individuals, showing you what real experience in fields you may find interesting looks and feels like. Bidaya is the perfect companion for anyone with a dream to succeed. With Bidaya, your future is defined by purpose.
